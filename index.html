Math Quiz

How to Play?

  • There are 25 questions that you must answer. They contain mathematical expressions of varying difficulty, starting from addition and subtraction to multiplication and division.
  • You have 10 seconds to answer each question. When the time runs out, the correct answer will be revealed without an addition to your score. Think fast and act fast!
  • Correct answers are worth 4 points. Incorrect answers or blank answers (due to running out of time) won't deduct score.
  • When you have answered all questions, you'll be graded based on the amount of correct answers you got. There'll also be a timer that tracks how much time you've spent overall and the average of your time spent on each question.
